OEM/ODM

OEM/ODM

Ruizhi is a cosmetic packaging manufacturer in Guangzhou, China, focused on OEM and ODM squeeze tubes, airless pump tubes, and cosmetic bottles for skincare, makeup, hair and personal care brands selling in Europe, North America, the Middle East and beyond.

OEM & ODM — what we do

  • OEM: your artwork, formula positioning and brand guidelines; we match tube or bottle structure, cap, barrier layers and decoration.
  • ODM: development support for new shapes, applicators (brush, roller, sponge, ceramic tips), dual chambers and massage functions.

Materials & sustainability

PE and laminated tubes (ABL/PBL), PCR, sugarcane and kraft-paper composite options, aluminum tubes, plus PET/PETG/HDPE bottles — for brands that need both performance and credible green claims.

Decoration & quality

Offset, silk screen, hot stamping and metallic finishes; consistency for wholesale and repeat orders. In-house tooling coordination and sample validation before mass production.
